About Slot Simulator

Hello! Welcome to the Slot Simulator!

If you're a slot technician, casino manager, or just a general user who would like to take a look at how slot odds work, then you've come to the right place my friend!

In here, you can test all sorts of odds and values with our own digital slot machine. Plus, with our net profit and net loss reports, you can even analyze what combinations work best for whom; house or player! The testing is all up to you, the simulator merely facilitates it.

Also, you can add as many credits as you like in the upper navigation bar so you can keep on testing. Note that when you configure either the odds or the values, the paytable will auto-update for you to reflect your changes to the machine! Just press the "?" icon at any point to view the current paytable.

Enjoy!
